Teach English as a foreign language abroad and online with The TEFL Org.

With huge demand for English teachers across the globe there are a wide range of opportunities available once you’re a TEFL certified teacher. Work in public schools, language centres, summer schools and universities with students of all ages and abilities. Travel to countries such as Spain, Japan, and Italy, or stay right at home and teach English online.

All you need to get TEFL certified is fluency in English - no previous experience or qualifications required. Our courses will teach you everything you need to know to start teaching English for the first time.

With more accreditation than any other provider, our qualifications are recognised internationally. You’ll join a community of over 140,000 TEFL Org teachers and embark on a TEFL adventure of your very own.

But first, you need to get qualified. Our best-selling 120-hour Premier TEFL course and Ofqual-regulated Level 5 TEFL course offer flexible self-paced online study. All of our courses include the support of an experienced TEFL tutor, lifetime access to our exclusive Jobs Centre, and more!
